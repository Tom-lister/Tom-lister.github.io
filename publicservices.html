DarkCorp has built...

Local Farms

Located in the center of Camellia City, DarkCorp™ Local Farms provide a variety of crops to the local residents, completely free of charge.

A Sheep-O-Matic™ is also under construction, which will provide residents with a renewable supply of wool forever.

Mob Farm

Using the latest in mob-herding technology, the DarkCorp™ Mob Farm provides residents of Camellia City with a bountiful supply of every mob drop they could ever need.

The Mob Farm's ability to generate gunpowder is its most noteable feature, as residents who purchase elytra will have a free and steady supply of firework rockets.

XP Farm

The latest XP Farm utilises the classic BigRiz™ design, providing the user with a steady stream of endermen which can all be killed in a single hit.

This contraption is invaluable to all users of the mending enchantment, and provides plenty of levels for a one time fee.

And there's much more to come!
